Research

Research Directions

From single-modality self-supervised learning to multimodal foundation models.

Discrete Representations for Cross-Modal Alignment

Codebook and vector quantization methods that learn structured visual dictionaries, bridging continuous image features with discrete symbolic spaces for vision-language alignment.

CoBooMDiSSECTMICCAIECAI

LLM-Driven 3D Medical Segmentation

Foundation models that accept natural language prompts to segment anatomical structures and pathologies in 3D CT/MRI volumes.

Foundation ModelsLLM + Vision3D

Multimodal Fusion & Learning

Frameworks that jointly learn from medical images, physiological signals (ECG, EEG), radiology reports, and clinical metadata for unified patient representations.

MultimodalVLMsRobSurv

Dense Semantic Alignment via Optimal Transport

Reformulating SSL alignment as optimal transport for fine-grained feature correspondence applicable to vision-language matching.

OTCXRWACV

Cross-Domain Temporal Representations

Multi-frequency bootstrapping for time-series signals, generalizing SSL across imaging and temporal modalities.

IEEE TNNLSTime-Series

Robust Embedding Regularization

Multi-level variance-covariance regularization preventing representational collapse while preserving global and fine-grained detail.
